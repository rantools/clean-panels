# N-Panel Renaming

With Clean Panels, you have the flexibility to rename tabs in the N-Panel to suit your preferences. Whether you prefer a shorter name or a complete change, you can easily achieve it.

**Getting Started**

1. Enable 'Show Advanced Options' in the addon preferences.
2. Two new sections will appear: one for N-Panel Reordering and one for N-Panel Renaming.
3. Navigate to the Renaming section, where you'll find a list of addons with their current tab names.
4. Simply double-click the tab name to modify it.
5. Press confirm to apply your changes or restart Blender.
